## Shared Play

Shared Play lets you upload a temporary playable package for a track and create a link that others
can use to listen in a browser or compatible Spectralis client.

Shared Play links are private by obscurity, not account authentication. Anyone with the link may be
able to access the session until it expires or is removed. Do not upload or share content through
Shared Play unless you are allowed to share it with anyone who receives the link.

Shared Play sessions are created with a 12-hour expiration and may be removed earlier for abuse,
security, operational, legal, or rights-related reasons.
